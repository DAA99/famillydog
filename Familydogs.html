ALPERT FAMILY'S DOGS

It has been our privilege and pleasure to share our home with thirteen dogs during the course of our almost six-decade marriage. For the past 20 or so years we have been very involved with our local humane society and became aware that some wonderful animals are overlooked for adoption because of their advanced age or health issues. Therefore, in an attempt to give these "unadoptable" dogs a good life in their remaining years, we decided to adopt senior dogs or those with chronic health conditions. Herein is an introduction to each of our unique canine companions. I hope you enjoy "meeting" each of them.
