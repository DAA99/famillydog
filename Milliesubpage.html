MILLIE

Born: unknown (circa 1993)

Adopted: 05/11/2007

Died: 06/07/2008
A year after adopting Max, and after Max died, we adopted Millie, a longhaired Dachshund who was about 14 years old. She touched my heart, as she was incredibly sweet and gentle. Unfortunately, despite Ira's objection, I listened to the vet's advice to tube-feed Millie to give her liver time to regenerate. I finally realized that the tube feeding was torture for her and that I was not prolonging her life but instead was prolonging her death. She was euthanized to put an end to her suffering and Ira keeps Millie's ashes in a cabinet near his dresser.
