CHARLIE II

Born: circa 11/2000

Adopted: 01/31/2005

Died: 06/17/2014
One day at lunch next door to the old Woods Humane Society's kennels, Ira told me to go look at a dog being held there. The dog Ira wanted me to see was charcoal grey/black with cream-colored markings on his face and paws; was 4-years old; and was named Charlie! Although he was a bearded collie, not a Lhasa Apsos, I know that my beloved first Charlie, who had such a short life, had meant for us to have this Charlie and so we adopted him. He came to live with us, joining Jack and Molly. Charlie was beautiful, but had a very bad temper. I thought I could control his behavior, but after living with us for nine years and being bitten by him, I finally realized that Charlie posed a danger to people that I was incapable of preventing. He was euthanized at age 14. Ira keeps Charlie's ashes in a cabinet near his dresser.
